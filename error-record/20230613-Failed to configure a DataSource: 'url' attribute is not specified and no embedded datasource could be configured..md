![image-20230613222245690](images/image-20230613222245690.png)

# 解决办法

- 将主启动类的注解@SpringBootApplication当中排除掉DataSourceAutoConfiguration配置文件
- 修改为@SpringBootApplication(exclude = DataSourceAutoConfiguration.class)
- 解决问题
